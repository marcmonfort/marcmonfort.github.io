The IMOL symposium series has a rich history of successful editions and workshops. Here you can find links to previous years and connect with the wider IMOL community.

## Previous Editions

- **IMOL 2022** in Tübingen, Germany, organized by Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems [webpage](https://2022.imol-conf.org)
- **IMOL 2019** in Frankfurt, Germany, organized by Institute for Advanced Studies (FIAS) [webpage](https://2019.imol-conf.org)
- **IMOL 2017** in Rome, Italy, organized by Institute of Cognitive Sciences and Technologies (ISTC-CNR)

- **IMOL 2009** in Venice, Italy, organized by Institute of Cognitive Sciences and Technologies (ISTC-CNR)

## IMOL Community

The IMOL symposium is part of the broader IMOL Community—a network of researchers interested in intrinsically motivated and open-ended learning.
