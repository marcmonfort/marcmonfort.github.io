## IMOL 2027 Code of Conduct

IMOL 2027 is committed to providing a welcoming and supportive environment for all participants. We are dedicated to creating a symposium where everyone feels safe, valued, and respected.

### Our Commitment

The IMOL 2027 symposium is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, age, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, or technical level. We do not tolerate harassment of conference participants in any form, and sexual language and imagery is not appropriate for any venue.

### Expected Behavior

All participants (including, but not limited to, attendees, speakers, organizers, volunteers, and sponsors) are expected to:

- **Be respectful** of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **Be inclusive** and welcoming to all participants
- **Use welcoming and inclusive language**
- **Respect physical and emotional boundaries**
- **Listen actively** to others without interruption
- **Participate authentically** and mindfully
- **Alert organizers** if you notice a violation of this code of conduct

### Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Offensive comments, jokes, or slurs related to any aspect of identity
- Unwelcome sexual attention or physical contact
- Threats of violence
- Sustained disruption of talks, panels, or other events
- Recording or photographing people without consent
- Violations of privacy or confidentiality

### Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior from any community participant will not be tolerated. Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behavior is expected to comply immediately.

If a participant engages in unacceptable behavior, the organizers may take appropriate action, including:

- Warning the individual
- Requesting the individual to leave the venue
- Banning the individual from future IMOL events
- Reporting the behavior to relevant authorities when appropriate

### Reporting

If you are subject to unacceptable behavior or witness such behavior, please contact an organizer immediately.

**Contact Email:** [imol2027@aalto.fi](mailto:imol2027@aalto.fi)

All reports will be treated with confidentiality and will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

### Support Resources

If you need support or have questions about the code of conduct, please reach out to the organizers. We are committed to creating a safe and respectful environment for everyone.

### Acknowledgments

This Code of Conduct is adapted from best practices in the research community and is based on similar codes used by other conferences and symposia. We are grateful for the collective wisdom of the academic community in creating inclusive and respectful spaces.

---

By participating in IMOL 2027, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct. We look forward to welcoming you to a positive and productive symposium.
